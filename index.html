About Me

Hey! I’m the creator of DrawScreen. I built this because I was amazed by how useful it is to simply sketch over your screen — whether for teaching, brainstorming, or just doodling for fun. My goal is to keep this tool lightweight, free, and easy to use. 🚀

FAQ

❓ How do I start drawing?

Use CTRL+ALT+Z to toggle the drawing window. Then use hotkeys inside it.

🎨 How do I change brush size?

Press O to increase, I to decrease brush size.

🌈 Can I pick a color?

Yes! Use CTRL+SHIFT+C to pick a color directly from the screen.

💾 How big is the app?

The installer is ~50MB because it includes all required libraries to run smoothly on any PC.
