FAQ

🖥️ What platforms are supported?

Currently Windows only. Linux/Mac support may come later. Or they are already here, I don't really know, I don't hace a Mac :P

⌨️ What are the hotkeys?

CTRL+ALT+Z → Toggle drawing
CTRL+ALT+X → Quit safely
O → Increase brush size
I → Decrease brush size
CTRL+SHIFT+C → Pick color
